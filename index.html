I am Moritz Kröger, a German front-end developer currently working as User Interface Developer at Razorfish in Berlin.

I have a strong focus and enthusiasm in JavaScript (ES6 ♥) as well as other emerging technologies from the front-end stack. Front-end architecture, web standards, performance and open source are the keywords which get me excited.

Since I started working as a front-end developer, I had the privilege to work with companies such as Audi, Here (Nokia), Coca-Cola, Nike, Axa, Deutsche Bahn, McDonald's, Demodern and more.
