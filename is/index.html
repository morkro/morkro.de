Who is that guy anyway?

Hello! — I'm Moritz Kröger, a German front-end developer currently working as User Interface Developer at Razorfish Germany in Berlin.

I have a strong focus and enthusiasm in JavaScript (especially ES6) as well as other emerging technologies from the front-end stack.
Since I started working as a front-end developer, I had the privilege to work with companies such as Audi, Here (Nokia), Coca-Cola, Axa, Deutsche Bahn, McDonald's, Demodern and more.

I love the web and every aspect of it. I recently started writing to share more of my experiences. In my spare time I like to fiddle around with different technologies, contribute to open source projects and a few month ago I started working with Oculus Rift.

So now you know who I am. Who are you?
