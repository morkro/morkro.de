Hey there!

I am Moritz Kröger (that's the real life version of morkro), a German front-end developer who lives in Berlin. Although Germans typically have a thing for extra long compound words, I am a friend of short functions.

I recently started sharing my developer experience on this blog. Most of my spare time is dedicated to fiddling around with different technologies: I enjoy trying out new concepts, experimenting with Virtual Reality and making contributions to open source projects. When I am not on the interwebs, I can be found traveling throughout Europe and the world trying to catch 'em all.

In the world of web development my focus is on JavaScript (ES6 ♥) and other emerging technologies from the front-end stack. #ecmascript, #frontend-architecture, #web-standards, #performance and #opensource are the hashtags which get me excited.

Ever since I started doing all this, I had the opportunity to work with great companies such as Audi, Here (Nokia), Coca-Cola, Nike, Axa, Deutsche Bahn, McDonald's, Demodern and more.

I am currently working at Razorfish as User Interface Developer.
